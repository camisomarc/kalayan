KALAYAN

Two Worlds. One Seed. Many Hearts.

When a typhoon tears through Elias Mercado’s world, a mysterious Traveler gives him a glowing seed—key to another world. Pulled into Kalayan, a realm of stars, spirits, ancient guardians, and living magic, Elias must survive, belong, and protect both worlds from a rising darkness.

Elias Mercado

A boy from Earth. Curious. Kind. Carrying more than he knows.

The Traveler

He comes when the world is breaking. Calm in the eye of the storm. He does not seek power. He seeks connection.

The Seed

An ancient living spark of light. Not a weapon—a responsibility that chooses the one with the heart willing to protect.

The Power of Kapwa

Magic in Kalayan does not come from within. It flows between.

The Core Truth

Relationships are the source of all magic. Trust, presence, vulnerability, loyalty, love, and unity awaken the thread that connects all things.

Stronger bonds mean stronger magic. More hearts create greater possibilities.

Earth and Kalayan

Earth is a world of storms, struggle, and resilient hearts. Kalayan is a realm of stars and spirit, where ancient guardians keep balance and the light of Kapwa binds all things.
